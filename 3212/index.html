CSCI 3212, Fall 2020 Edition

Hello! Welcome to the Fall 2020 edition of the Algorithms course. I am substituting for Rahul Simha this semester while he is on sabbatical. I very much look forward to teaching this course.
Course Structure:
Like all undergraduate courses for the Fall of 2020, this course will be held online. The course will meet Tuesday and Thursday from 11:10 -- 12:25.
Textbook:
The course will follow the following amazing, free, online textbook: Algorithms, by Jeff Erickson. You are explicitly allowed to download, copy, and share this book. Reading assignments below relate to this book.
